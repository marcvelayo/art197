Bio

Marc Velayo was born in Mandaluyong, Philippines in 1997. He immigrated to Santa Clara, California in 2012. He's currently pursuing his Bachelors of Fine Arts in Art, Concentration in Digital Media at San Jose State University. Marc's work features various mediums centering around the importance of mental health and social justice. He creates illustrations, paintings and is highly proficient in graphic design. Marc has competed in several competitions such as the Adobe Creative Jam, where his team placed 1st for a UI/UX competition against several CSU schools and 2nd for BORDERS: a Series of Short- Films. Marc's work is currently featured around the city of Oakland, as he placed 2nd for BRIDGEGOOD's Inspire Oakland 2020 Digital Media Competition. In 2019, he was awarded as a Forbes Under 30 Scholar, and was given the Robert Romero Visual Award from the Santa Clara Youth Focus Inc. in 2016.
